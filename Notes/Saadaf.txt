Jute furniture brings substantial economic benefits to Bangladesh's economy by
creating value in the jute sector, supporting rural employment and enhanching
export potential. Jute as a locally sourced and renewable material, it reduces
production cost while supporting eco-friendly innovation. According to Islam and
Ali (2018), by creating employment for rural artisans, perticulerly women, jute-
based industries are contributing to the development of inclusive economy. Moreover,
the emerging demand for sustainable products highlights jute furniture as an
export option with increasing amount of foreign exchange potential(Islam & Ali, 2018).
Furniture production with jute can promote diversification of Bangladesh's export base
and stregnthen sustainable economic development.
